Hi, I'm Karl

I'm working as a software developer at hot coffee apps in Bremen, which I co-founded in 2011.

I studied Media Informatics and Digital Media at the University of Applied Sciences in Bremen and NYU-Poly in New York.

I like to code and do user experience and screen design for mobile applications. My preferred tools are Xcode, OmniGraffle, TextMate and Photoshop.

In my spare time I like to watch my favorite soccer team Werder Bremen, go running or spend time with my friends.

Projects

I've been working on different projects and with several technologies in the past. You can find a few recent ones on our company's website at hotcoffeeapps.com.

At the university I have been working on betaville, a system to support collaborative urban city-planning by integrating mobile and stationary multitouch devices. More recently I'm doing research on visual programming languages for tablet devices to provide an enganging learning experience for children when making their first steps towards programming.

If you're a student in Bremen, check out my app BreMensa!
